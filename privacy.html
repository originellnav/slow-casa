Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Slow Casa ("we", "us", "our") operates slowcasa.com. This page explains how we collect, use and protect any information you provide when using our website.

Information we collect

We collect your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter via Beehiiv. We do not collect any other personal information unless you contact us directly, in which case we may retain your name and email address to respond to your enquiry.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous data including pages visited, time on site and general location. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

How we use your information

Your email address is used solely to send you The Slow Signal newsletter. We will not sell, share or rent your email address to any third party. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies for Google Analytics and to remember preferences such as newsletter dismissals. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third party services

We use the following third party services: Beehiiv for newsletter delivery, Google Analytics for site analytics, Airtable for content management, Cloudinary for image hosting, and Mapbox for mapping. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. Upon unsubscribing, your data is removed from our mailing list in accordance with Beehiiv's data retention policies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at hello@slowcasa.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@slowcasa.com.
